\symdecl*{Russell-Myhill Paradox}
\begin{sparagraph}[style=symdoc]
  The \definame{Russell-Myhill Paradox} is a paradox discovered by \sr{Russell}{Bertrand Russell} which states that two systems of propositions with the same so-called \emph{logical product} are not necessarily equal.
\end{sparagraph}
