    Let $A, B$ be classes.
    The Cartesian product of $A$ and $B$ is
    \[\fclass{\tuple{a,b}}{\text{$a \in A$ and $b \in B$}}.\]

    Let the product of $A$ and $B$ stand for the Cartesian product of $A$ and $B$.
    Let $\prod{A,B}$ stand for the Cartesian product of $A$ and $B$.
  \end{fdefinition*}
